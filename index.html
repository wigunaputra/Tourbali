About us

Who We Are

Hi, my name is Nyoman. I have more than 10 years of experience as a local driver in Bali, helping travelers from all over the world explore the island safely and comfortably. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of guiding guests to discover not only Bali’s famous landmarks but also its hidden gems that most visitors never get to see.

With this passion, I started Newman Bali Tour — a place where travelers can easily book trusted local drivers and enjoy a journey that feels personal, friendly, and stress-free. For me, being a driver is not just about transportation; it’s about creating memorable experiences, sharing local culture, and making sure every guest feels at home in Bali.

When you travel with me or my team, you’re not just booking a driver — you’re choosing a companion who cares about your journey as much as you do.
